Impossible Quiche Recipes

Ingredients

  1. 125g ham, chopped
  2. 1 small onion, finely chopped
  3. 1 1/2 cups (180g) grated Devondale Tasty Cheese Block (500g)
  4. 1/3 cup (50g) self-raising flour
  5. Salt & freshly ground pepper, to season
  6. 4 eggs
  7. 1 1/2 cups (375ml) milk

3 Method Steps

Step 1

Preheat oven to 200Â°C. Grease a 5 cup capacity pie dish.

Step 2

Combine ham, onion, cheese, flour, salt and pepper in a medium bowl. Scatter over base of dish.

Step 3

Whisk eggs and milk together in a large jug and pour over ham mixture. Cook for about 40 minutes or until puffed and golden. Cool slightly. Serve warm or cold.
